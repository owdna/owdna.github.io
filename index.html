COVID-19 UPDATE

Currently, the neighborhood is organizing to help neighbors in need. OWDNA is conducting a SURVEY of needs and we invite you to participate. For those who would like to help out, we invite you to SIGN ON as a "Block Coordinator". Our Block coordinators are asked to be attentive to local needs on their chosen block. If you already know a number of people on your block, you may be an ideal candidate. Please follow this link to SIGN ON if you are willing to help coordinate assistance for your local neighbors.

Upcoming Events

October 22nd, 2020 @ 7pm - OWDNA Annual Meeting & Fall Board Meeting
We are looking forward to our virtual annual meeting via Zoom! We will hear from a guest speaker (from Preservation Durham about Durham history.) Then, we will conduct annual board elections, per our bylaws. Immediately after (at 8 pm), the board will hold its regular quarterly meeting. OWD neighbors/friends are invited to join the call at any point. The board will offer a slate of candidates, as we have done in the past. At the board meeting, there will be a time for neighbor input and neighbors may comment on any items on the agenda. More information about these meetings will be posted as it becomes available.

OWDNA Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) Board meets the fourth Thursdays of January, April, July, and October, at 7:00pm. See Our Upcoming Events for the next meeting date and zoom details.

Current Board members for 2020 are: Fred Broadwell, Beth Dray, David Eklund, Charles Kacir, Chelsie Mitchell, Beth Plonk, Cathy Saylor, Vicky Welch, John Wood. You can reach the group at owdna-board[at]googlegroups.com
