OWDNA Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) Board meets the last Thursday of every month at 7:00pm. Join us to get more involved in your neighborhood!

Current Board members are: Ginger Blubaugh (vice president), Beth Dray (treasurer), Jesus Gutierrez, Simeon Furman, Beth Plonk, Dan Welch and Vicky Welch. You can reach the group at owdna-board[at]googlegroups.com
