Living in Old West Durham

Important Information for Residents

Trash Days

The city of Durham collects garbage weekly on Thursdays. Recycling is collected only every other week. For more information on the specific schedules and other information about the city's waste management services, see their information here:

Durham Solid Waste Management

Police Districts

Old West Durham sits in two different police districts (2 and 3), and is covered by beats numbered 213 and 312. See here for more information about the Durham Police Department.

Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) meets once a month on the last Thursday of the month, 7:00PM at 2650 Lawndale Avenue. Join us to get more involved in your neighborhood.

Your board members are Nicole Roberge (President), Beth Plonk, Dylan Young, Eric Heidt, John Killeen, KrishaSong Parkey Miller, Shaun Pack, and Vicky Welch
